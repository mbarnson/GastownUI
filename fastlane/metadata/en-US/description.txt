Gas Town UI - Your AI Agent Command Center

Control your fleet of autonomous coding agents with voice commands and a beautiful dashboard. Gas Town UI brings enterprise-grade AI orchestration to your pocket.

VOICE-FIRST CONTROL
- Push-to-talk voice commands for hands-free agent management
- Natural language queries: "Why is the convoy stuck?" "Which polecats are idle?"
- Multiple voice personas: Mayor, Witness, Refinery, and more
- Local AI processing with LFM2.5 - your data stays on device

REAL-TIME MONITORING
- Track active convoys and work progress
- Monitor polecat (worker) status across all rigs
- View blocked issues and dependencies
- Activity feed with cost tracking

DEEP QUERY ANALYSIS
- Ask natural language questions about your agent fleet
- Get AI-powered insights without cloud dependencies
- Understand blockers, progress, and optimization opportunities

BUILT FOR DEVELOPERS
- Seamless integration with Git workflows
- Beads issue tracking at your fingertips
- tmux session management
- Cross-platform sync with desktop app

Gas Town UI is built on Tauri 2.0 for native performance and security. Your agent orchestration data never leaves your device.
